5) What is On-Ramps and Off-Ramps ??
- On-Ramps is the service that allows you to convert your traditional money like dollars into cryptocurrency.
- Off-Ramps is the service that allows you to convert your cryoto back to your traditional money.


6) Decentralized Exchanges (DEX) ??
- Let you to trade your tokens directly from your wallet that you own. Without any company being in the middle controlling anything.
- Uniswap is the largest DEX

Centralized Exhanges use the Order Books. Where buyers ans sellers place there orders that get matched up.
DEX instead used the Automatic Market Makers or AMMs and they work very differently. Instead of matching buyers with sellers, trades
happens with something called liquidity pool.

Liquidity Pool is the pool of tokens.
Someone who deposite tokens into the pool is called liquidity provider and they have deposite equal value of two different tokens


7) Decentralized Governance ??
- Here the token holders are the decision makes.

8) What is DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations) ??
- Its the smart contract that executes based on the votes.
- This are governance tokens that lets community to used for voting. 
- Any project or company that employes this decentralized governance is referred to DAO.

9) How DAO Works ??
- There is first proposal for any changes.
- There is voting period. Where token holders connect there wallet and cast there votes by signing blockchain transactions.
- The smart contract tracks the votes and how tokens back it. 
- More token someone votes the more weight there vote has. 
- When the voting end the smart contract tallies up all the results and if proposal passes then the changes will implemented.
- No CEO or Board can reverse this decision
