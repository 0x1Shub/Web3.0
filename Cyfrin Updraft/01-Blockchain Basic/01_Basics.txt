✅ Web3 Ecosystem – Categories & Explanations

Blockchain (Layer 1)
The base layer where transactions happen and smart contracts are executed.
Examples: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Avalanche.

Layer 2 & Layer 3 Scaling Solutions
Built on top of Layer 1 to improve speed and reduce fees without compromising security.
Examples: Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, zkSync, StarkNet.

Decentralized Oracle Networks
Connect real-world data (like price feeds, weather) to smart contracts.
Examples: Chainlink, Band Protocol, API3.

Smart Contract Languages & Frameworks
Tools to write and deploy smart contracts.
Languages: Solidity (Ethereum), Vyper, Rust (Solana), Move (Aptos, Sui).
Frameworks: Hardhat, Truffle, Foundry, Anchor (Solana).

Wallets
Store and manage private keys and interact with dApps.
Examples: MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Ledger, Phantom (Solana).

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) Protocols
Finance without banks: lending, borrowing, trading.
Examples: Uniswap, Aave, Compound, Curve.

NFT & Gaming Platforms
For NFTs and blockchain games.
Examples: OpenSea, Rarible, Axie Infinity.

Decentralized Storage
Store data off-chain in a decentralized way.
Examples: IPFS, Filecoin, Arweave, Storj.

Cross-Chain Bridges
Move assets between different blockchains.
Examples: Wormhole, Multichain, Hop Protocol.

Infrastructure & Node Providers
Enable developers to connect to blockchains easily without running their own nodes.
Examples: Infura, Alchemy, QuickNode, Moralis.

DAO Frameworks
Tools for creating Decentralized Autonomous Organizations.
Examples: Aragon, Snapshot, Gnosis Safe.

Identity & Authentication
Decentralized identities and logins.
Examples: ENS (Ethereum Name Service), Ceramic, Lit Protocol.
