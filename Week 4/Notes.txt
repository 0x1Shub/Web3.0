6) Accounts on Solana
- On the solana blockchain, an "Account" is a fundamental data structure used to store various types of information.
- Data Storage: Accounts on solana are used to store data required by programs (smart contracts) or to maintain state
- Lamports: Accounts hold a balance of Solana native cyptocurrency, lamports. Lamports are used pay for transaction fees and to rent the space that account occupies on the blockchain.
- Programs: On Solana, programs are special accounts that contain executable code. These accounts are distict from regular data accounts in that they are designed to be executable by blockchain when trigger by transaction.

7) Types of Accounts
- Wallet Account : Holds SOL (lamports)
- Data Account : Stores app data
- Program Account: Stores executable code
- Token Account (special case): Stores token balances (via Token Program)

When you are creating accounts in blockchain. You need to have some lamports in accounts otherwise it will close.

11) What is Rent on the solana blockchain ??
- Rent on Solana is the amount of SOL (stored as lamports) required to keep an account and its data stored on-chain.
Because on Solana:
Accounts store data
Validators must store that data on hardware
Storage costs resources

💰 Is It Paid Every Month?
Important beginner clarification:

Not like monthly house rent.

Usually what developers do:

Rent-Exempt Balance

They deposit enough SOL once so the account remains active.

That minimum amount is called rent-exempt minimum.

If account has enough balance:

✅ It stays alive
✅ No periodic deductions in normal developer experience

📌 Example

Suppose your app creates a data account needing 200 bytes.

Solana may require some SOL minimum (varies by network rules/tools).

You fund the account with that minimum.

Now the account can persist.

🔥 Why Does Solana Do This?

To prevent spam.

Without storage cost:

Someone could create millions of useless accounts and bloat the chain.

Rent requirement discourages junk data.

🧠 Where You’ll See This as a Developer

When using Anchor / Solana programs:

Creating PDA or account:

you allocate space
fund account with lamports
system program creates it

Example concepts:

space = 8 + data_size
payer funds rent-exempt minimum
🪙 Lamports Reminder

1 SOL = 1,000,000,000 lamports

Rent values are usually expressed in lamports.

⚙️ Common Account Types Needing Rent
App state accounts
User profile accounts
Token accounts
PDA accounts
Mint accounts
🔄 Can You Get It Back?

Often yes.

If account is closed properly, remaining lamports can be returned to a destination wallet.

That’s why many say it’s more like a refundable storage deposit than rent.

13) What is RPC Url ??
- An RPC URL is the web address of a blockchain node/server that lets your wallet, CLI, or app communicate with the blockchain.
- https://api.devnet.solana.com

🔥 Why RPC is Needed
Without RPC:
❌ Your wallet can’t read balances
❌ Can’t send transactions
❌ Can’t fetch account data
Because blockchain data must be accessed through nodes.

Mainnet: https://api.mainnet-beta.solana.com
Devnet: https://api.devnet.solana.com
Localhost: http://127.0.0.1:8899
