1) What is dapp ??
- A dApp (Decentralized Application) is an application whose backend logic runs on a blockchain or decentralized network
instead of being controlled entirely by a single centralized server.

2) Why it is called dapp ??
It is called a dApp because the "d" stands for decentralized.

3) What does the dapp do ??
A dapp request users to sign the transactions using their wallet.
So a dApp is not only for signing transactions, but signing is how users authorize the dApp to perform blockchain actions on their behalf.

4) Flow of dapp ??
- User comes to wallet.
- User finalises their transaction.
- Dapp requests approval from user wallet.
